# TPO Coach

A skill to help me stay focused as a TPO and not go crazy.

## What it does

This is a personal coaching skill for Claude. When I share a situation - a backlog decision, a customer signal, a RaP I am preparing - it challenges my thinking instead of just validating it.

It runs the Decision Filter before any backlog commitment, keeps me anchored to customer problems (not just Jira tickets), and flags when I have drifted too far inward.

## How to use

Start with SKILL.md. It contains the full coaching instructions, the Decision Filter, and the weekly rhythm check.

The skill references these files from the vault (not included here - they live locally):
- Resources/DoiT-TPO-Playbook.md - full PM framework library
- Resources/tpo-decision-filter.md - the complete Decision Filter
- Resources/tpo-ryan-guidance-jun2026.md - Ryan's guidance on inward/outward balance

## Core idea

The risk as a TPO is becoming very good at inward execution - Jira, Confluence, planning - while spending less time with the people experiencing the problems you are trying to solve. This skill keeps me honest.
